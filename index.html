Frequently Asked Questions

Most installations take 3-5 days depending on building height and complexity. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to building operations and can often work during off-hours or weekends if needed.
Garbage chutes must comply with NFPA 82, local fire codes, and building codes. We handle all permit applications and ensure full compliance with regulations including fire ratings, ventilation requirements, and safety features.
We recommend professional cleaning every 6-12 months for most buildings, with high-traffic buildings needing more frequent service. Regular cleaning prevents odors, pest issues, and maintains sanitary conditions.
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency repair services. Our response time is typically 2-4 hours for emergencies, and we carry common parts in our service vehicles for immediate repairs.
We provide a 5-year warranty on new installations covering materials and workmanship. Repair work comes with a 1-year warranty. Our maintenance customers receive extended warranty coverage on covered components.
